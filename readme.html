house rules • read before adopting

⚠️ IMPORTANT STUFF TO READ BEFORE PURCHASING⚠️

1. vinatge & pre-loved, not brand new.
please expect signs of age & wear: scuffs, scratches, marks on screens, dust in corners or viewfinders, yellowing plastic/rubber etc.
please note: we'll remove deteriorated/sticky rubber caps. we’ll always flag any bigger quirks in the listing.

2. testing & functionality.

each cam is tested for power-on, basic shooting, flash, zoom & playback; once these functions work, we deem the cam FUNCTIONAL (any issues with these functions will be stated in the item description). we do not guarantee any other functions.
these are old electronics; please treat them gently, expect small personality traits & note we cannot guarantee their longevity & there is a possibility of them malfunctioning over time. note: battery capacity decreases over time and may need to be replacedplease don't hesitate to reach out if you need help sourcing for replacements!

3. physical condition
we clean & inspect each cam thoroughly and will list any major defectsdefects other than the standard signs of age and wear like scuffs, scratches, marks on screens, dust in corners or viewfinders, yellowing plastic/rubber etc. in the item description.

4. no refunds or returns, unless...
we are confident in our testing protocols so you can be assured that all cams listed FUNCTIONAL have the functionsi.e. power-on, basic shooting, flash, zoom and playback mentioned above, & any major defectsdefects other than the standard signs of age and wear like scuffs, scratches, marks on screens, dust in corners or viewfinders, yellowing plastic/rubber etc. will be listed.
so we don't accept returns or issue refunds UNLESS you provide us with a full unboxing video which includes you showing either (a) that the functions tested for (see above) do not work or (b) that there are any major defects in the physical condition of the cam, AND that that we did not mention the non-functionality or major defect.

5. what's in the box?
our cams usually come with their battery unless stated otherwise OR the cam uses generic batteries. nothing else is included unless the listing specifically states so, and if we include any chargers, accessories, etc., we don't guarantee their usability/compatibility, these are just freebies included because we like you

6. memory cards & files.
if a cam ships with a memory card, we'll format / wipe it before sending. anyway please ALWAYS format your memory cards before shooting, or you risk losing your pics T.T

7. first come first served
if you're interested, make your order and pay fast! we have limited stock & some of these cams are no longer in production. we sell to whoever orders and makes payment first!

8. colours, vibes & sample pics.
colours may look slightly different across screens and lighting. any sample photos or videos just show the cam’s general vibe & capabilities – they’re not a promise that every shot will look exactly the same.

8. safety stuff.
don’t open the body or try to repair electronics yourself unless you know what you’re doing. keep batteries away from heat/water & remove them if you’re not using the cam for a while to avoid leaks. cams generally don't do well with drops, knocks, water, heat, extreme cold, etc. just. please treat these senior citizens with care :')

7. not sponsored, not affiliated.
all product names, logos and trademarks belong to their respective owners. hippocampercams is an independent project that's not affiliated with any brand mentioned.

shop notes – Notepad

ordering.
DMs are first-come-first-served. i’ll hold a cam for a short agreed window once we’ve confirmed details – after that it may go back up for adoption.

payment.
default is paynow / bank transfer in SGD. please always wait for confirmation + final total (including delivery, if any) before sending payment.

delivery / meetups.
local SG: options usually include normal mail, tracked mail or meetup at agreed MRT stations. details will be in each listing or confirmed over DM.

packaging.
cams are wrapped snugly in bubble wrap / padding and boxed wherever possible. i try to reuse clean packing materials to be a little kinder to the planet.

DOA window.
if your cam arrives completely not working (DOA), please contact me within 48 hours with clear pics / videos so we can troubleshoot or work something out together.

expectations.
these are nostalgic toys first, serious workhorses second. if you need mission-critical gear, please consider buying new – hippocampercams is mainly for fun, vibes and memories.
